What we do

At Maliard, we create opportunities at the intersection of Africa's resources and global markets. With a local presence in key African regions and operations in the UAE, we specialize in sourcing, trade financing, and logistics that connect businesses across borders.

As an African-founded company, we understand the value of being close to the ground—building partnerships that are rooted in trust, transparency, and a shared vision for sustainable growth.

We are a startup with corporate experience, blending agility with discipline to deliver practical solutions today while shaping stronger supply chains for tomorrow.

Maliard is open for business, and we believe the future is built through collaboration.
