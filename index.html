Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about precious metals and minerals sourcing from Africa and UAE.

Maliard sources precious metals and minerals from established suppliers across Africa and globally, leveraging our strategic presence and operations in UAE to connect African resources with international markets.

We provide comprehensive trade finance solutions including working capital facilities, letters of credit, and international trade financing specifically designed for precious metals and minerals transactions between Africa, UAE, and global markets.

Our logistics network spans from Africa through UAE to global destinations, providing secure transportation, storage, and distribution services specifically designed for precious metals and minerals with full regulatory compliance and insurance coverage.

Africa offers direct access to the continent's rich precious metals resources, while UAE provides strategic access to global trading markets. Our presence across both regions creates an optimal corridor from source to international buyers, combining local expertise with global reach.
